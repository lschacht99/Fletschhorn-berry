History

A mountain property with a century behind it.

The story behind the property gives the stay its character.

1906
Early mountain hospitality begins.

A family builds mountain inns in Saas-Fee for the first travellers coming to the village.

1970s
The property grows.

The Dütsch family develops Fletschhorn into a respected alpine property.

1994
Swiss culinary history.

Irma Dütsch becomes the first Swiss woman to be awarded a Michelin star.

Now
A renewed private chalet.

The story continues as a private alpine base for groups, events, and whole-property stays.
