Useful details

Before you send the request.

Keep the page practical. Guests should know what happens after they click, without reading a long sales page.

Is this for the whole property?

Yes. The booking flow is designed for private whole-property stays at Fletschhorn.

What happens after I click “Book my stay now”?

The request is sent through the Worker with your dates, contact details, guest count, quote data when available, and stay notes.

Can I book events or retreats here?

Yes. Select the stay type and add notes about the event, food plan, or setup needs.

Is the price final?

The Worker quote is treated as an estimate unless the backend returns a confirmed checkout or booking link.
