Where it is

Above Saas-Fee, in the Valais Alps.

The property is positioned above the village in a pine-forest setting, with mountain views, fresh air, and a softer rhythm than the center of town. It is close enough for village access, but the feeling is calmer and more natural.

In the Swiss AlpsA high-mountain destination in Valais, surrounded by peaks, trails, snow, forest, and open views.
Near the villageSaas-Fee’s shops, lifts, restaurants, activities, and arrival points stay reachable without making the house feel central or noisy.
