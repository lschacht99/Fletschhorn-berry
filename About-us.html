The heritage

A Saas-Fee address with more than a century behind it.

The depth of Fletschhorn comes from the life of the place: early mountain tourism, the growth of Saas-Fee, culinary ambition, wine culture, and generations of guests drawn to this quiet position above the village.

1906

A mountain story that began with the first travellers to Saas-Fee.

1906
The story begins with Saas-Fee’s early travellers.

A family builds mountain inns in Saas-Fee to welcome the first tourists coming to the village. This is where the Fletschhorn story starts.

1951
Saas-Fee opens further to the world.

The first car reaches Saas-Fee, and the number of guests grows as the village develops into an international ski destination.

1970s
The Dütsch family grows the property.

Fletschhorn develops into a place with a strong hospitality and culinary reputation, expanding toward its current size and presence.

1980s
Markus Neff shapes a mountain destination.

A young chef joins the restaurant and helps turn Fletschhorn into a distinctive address high in the mountains.

1994
Irma Dütsch enters Swiss culinary history.

Irma Dütsch, owner and chef of the property, becomes the first Swiss woman to receive a Michelin star.

Today
The story continues through private gatherings.

The house now carries its history into a more intimate form: one group at a time, surrounded by forest, mountain views, and the quiet of Saas-Fee.

The house today

One alpine address, entirely held for your group.

Fletschhorn is for people who want privacy without losing the scale and comfort of a larger property. The rooms, shared areas, terraces, sauna, kitchen, and surrounding forest work together as one complete private alpine setting.

What makes it different

The experience begins when the group takes over the house.

Families, retreats, ski groups, wedding weekends, and private gatherings can settle in with the feeling of having their own mountain base above Saas-Fee. There is space to gather, space to step away, and enough structure to make the stay feel considered from arrival to departure.

The private kitchen concept

A professional kitchen, available to use your way.

At the heart of Fletschhorn is a professional kitchen for groups who want freedom around food. Use it independently, bring your own team, or ask us to help arrange a private chef or caterer for your event.

Food, your way

Your kitchen for the stay — with support available on request.

The chalet provides the space and the professional kitchen. Your group can cook for itself, bring its own chef, or request help arranging one of our available chefs or catering partners.


Chef and catering services are optional and quoted separately. This keeps the experience flexible: simple self-catering for some groups, a private chef for others, catering for events, or a mix depending on the schedule.


The food experience belongs to your group, your budget, your event, and the atmosphere you want to create.
